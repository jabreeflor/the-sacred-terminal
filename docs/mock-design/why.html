Why Sacred

The future of AI-assisted work isn't one model or one harness. It's many, working together.

Claude, Codex, Cursor, Gemini — each excels at different things. But today you're forced to pick one terminal, one workflow, one window at a time.

Sacred is the layer between your projects and the models. Sacred Sessions wires them together — every agent gets its own hosted process, organized under the same project tree, side by side.

No more juggling windows or copy-pasting context. Just a calm place to work, with the right agent one hover away.
